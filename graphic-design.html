Folktuga

Folktuga is a brand for which I created a visual identity.


An important part of my work for this project was the creation of a brand book and mock-ups of how the visual identity could be used. This was my first time working on a project of this size, and I learned a lot from it.

WEKO

I created the branding for a Danish start-up that helps people work together on housing projects. To achieve this I mainly talked to stakeholders, including potential customers and industry experts, to understand their needs. I also did market research to spot trends and gaps.


With this information I developed a brand that communicated the mission and values of Weko. This included designing the logo, creating marketing materials, and crafting a brand identity.

Workable

I created the branding for a start-up focussed on providing a community for workspaces abroad. This process mainly included conducting a lot of tests to find the branding that meets the needs of the end-customer the most.


I ended up delivering a logo and the brand identity for Workable, which they used for the company and their website!

DOify

DOify is a project that links people that like handmade projects and people that need to get rid of waste.


Since this was a start-up, the main focus of my work was providing the branding, creating a prototype of the webpage and making a marketing structure.

Plan'nGo

Plan'nGo is a social media platform for people looking to save and plan concerts and other events.


Since this was a newly-created start-up, the primary focus of my work was on creating low-fidelity prototypes with which I could test if people wanted to use the app. To achieve this, I programmed a website with Bubble and organized a marketing campaign.
